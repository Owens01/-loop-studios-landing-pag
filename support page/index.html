Loop Studio Support

Welcome to Loop Studio's support page! We're here to help you with any issues you might encounter while enjoying our games.

Here are some resources you can use to find answers:
  • FAQs: out our Frequently Asked Questions to see if your question has already been answered. We cover a wide range of topics, including account creation, troubleshooting technical problems, and billing inquiries.
  • Knowledge Base: Our knowledge base is a searchable collection of articles that provide detailed information on a variety of topics related to our games and services.
  • Search Bar: Use the search bar at the top of this page to find specific articles or keywords within our support content.
If you can't find an answer to your question in the resources above, you can contact us directly:
  • Support Ticket: Submit a support ticket through our online portal. A member of our support team will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
  • Live Chat: If you prefer real-time assistance, you can chat with a support representative through our live chat option.

Here are some additional tips for getting help:
  • Be clear and concise: When submitting a support ticket or contacting us through live chat please be clear and concise about the issue you're experiencing. The more information you can provide, the better we can assist you.
  • Include screenshots or videos (if applicable):If you're encountering a technical problem, it can be helpful to include screenshots or videos that illustrate the issue.
  • Be patient: Our support team works hard to resolve all inquiries as quickly and efficiently as possible. However, during peak times, there may be a slight delay in response.

We appreciate your patience and understanding. We're committed to providing you with the best possible gaming experience
